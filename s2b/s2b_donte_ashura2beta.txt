This is Ashura the Hedgehog 2 (Beta). This is my first ROM hack. It's a basic palette hack. Also Title Cards.

What you will need:

A Sega Genesis Emulator

A Sonic 2 Beta ROM in binary format

An IPS Patcher (Segatool, Stealthpatch,etc.)


What's been done:

Palettes of every Zone have been changed

Sonic's palette was changed

Title Cards (by Esrael)
